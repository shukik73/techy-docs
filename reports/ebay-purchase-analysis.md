### Key Insights for Scout Agent
1. **Screens are the #1 category** — 27% of all orders. Scout should prioritize screen searches.
2. **Motherboards are #2** — higher avg price ($102), bigger margin opportunity.
3. **Most screens are under $100** — sweet spot is $50–$100 range.
4. **Repeat sellers = trusted sources** — aqscreenlcd, lcdtech889 should be preferred.
5. **Bliss Computers** appears for premium touch assemblies ($150–$200 range).
6. **Full laptop buys are rare but high-value** — $150–$1,700 range, for resale or parts harvesting.
7. **MacBook parts** (logic boards, screens, palmrests) are a significant subcategory.
8. **TV parts** are low volume — deprioritize in Scout.
